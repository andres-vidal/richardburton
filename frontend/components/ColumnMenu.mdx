# Column menu

The **Columns** control: a button that opens a popover to show or hide the
table's toggleable columns. Hiding a column simply stops rendering its cells —
there is no in-place collapse animation to lag on long lists. The title columns
always stay; only attributes flagged toggleable appear in the popover.

## Behaviour

- **Toggle a column** — each row is a checkbox-style toggle button
  (`aria-pressed`); pressed means the column is shown.
- **Badge** — when any column is hidden, the trigger shows a `shown/total`
  count.
- **Show all** — restores every column.
- **Positioning** — floating-ui anchors the popover below the trigger
  (`bottom-end`, 4px offset) and keeps it on screen: it flips above near the
  viewport bottom, shifts back in near the left edge, and follows the trigger on
  scroll (`autoUpdate`). These are asserted by geometry in the story play tests.
- **Dismiss** — a pointer press outside the popover closes it.
